ABOUT

My career in software development was started in 2004 when I was doing my bachelor's degree in computer science. It was still until 2015 aways working as a Java backend developer. OMG! Am I a veteran already?


After more than ten great years well lived in the IT field, an internal voice started to talk out loud, and I asked myself what else I would like o be. So I attended the call for adventure. I experimented with photography, watercolour and coffee - yes, I am a certificated barista -until I arrived in Canada in 2017.


Here I learned a new language, made new friends and started a new life. The only thing that never changed was my passion for software development, so I decided to come back to school.


In fall 2022, I will complete the Web Development - Front-End Design post-graduation program by George Brown College. Is it not awesome?


I believe in wold with equality, accessibility and diversity, and I want to make part of this transformation, improving how we interact with the world.
